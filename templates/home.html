User Manual

  • For Clients:
    1. Log in to your account using the provided credentials.
    2. View the list of available medical equipment and their details.
    3. Rent a piece of equipment by specifying the start and end date.
    4. Complete the transaction by following the payment instructions.
    5. View your rental history and payment status.
  • For Managers:
    1. Log in to your manager account with the given credentials.
    2. Update the inventory database by adding new equipment or updating existing ones.
    3. Take orders from clients over the phone or in person and generate receipts.
    4. Generate reports, including customer information, rented equipment, pending payments, and profit calculation.
